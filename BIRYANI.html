Facts about Biryani

  • Persian Roots: The word "biryani" comes from Persian words like birinj (rice) or biryan (fried before cooking).
  • Mughal Influence: Persian traders and Mughal emperors introduced it to the Indian subcontinent, where it was refined in royal kitchens
